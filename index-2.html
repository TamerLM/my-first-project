Mercedes Benz GLC 63 S AMG :

The Mercedes-AMG GLC 63 S is a high-performance SUV from Mercedes-Benz's AMG division, offering impressive power, luxury, and advanced technology. Here are its key specifications and features:


  • Engine & Performance:


    4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 ;503 hp (510 PS) and 700 Nm of torque

  • Chassis & Driving Dynamics:


    All-wheel drive (AMG 4MATIC+) with fully variable torque distribution,AMG Ride Control+ adaptive air suspension

  • Exterior & Interior:


    Aggressive AMG Panamericana grille,Sporty AMG body kit with aerodynamic enhancements,21-inch AMG wheels (varies by trim)

GLC power:

The GLC 63 S is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, delivering 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. This handcrafted AMG engine is known for its aggressive performance, rapid acceleration, and distinctive exhaust note. Paired with AMG's Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel-drive system and a nine-speed automatic transmission, the GLC 63 S offers a dynamic driving experience with exceptional handling and responsiveness.
