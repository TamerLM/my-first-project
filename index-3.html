Mercedes Benz GLC 43 AMG:



The Mercedes-AMG GLC 43 is a sportier version of the standard GLC, sitting below the high-performance GLC 63 S in the AMG lineup. It offers a balance of power, luxury, and daily usability.


  • Engine & Performance:


    2.0L turbocharged inline-4 with mild hybrid assistance Power Output: 416 hp (421 PS)

  • Chassis & Driving Dynamics:


    All-Wheel Drive (AMG 4MATIC) with 31:69 power distribution (rear-biased) AMG Ride Control adaptive suspension Rear-axle steering for improved handling and agility AMG braking system with ventilated discs

  • Exterior & Interior:


    AMG Panamericana grille for a sporty look 20-inch AMG wheels (optional 21-inch available) Quad exhaust tips for an aggressive sound MBUX infotainment system with AMG-specific graphics

The Mercedes-AMG GLC 43 is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends power, style, and practicality. Equipped with a 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 engine, it delivers over 400 horsepower and features AMG’s signature 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system for enhanced traction and handling. Its sporty design includes an aggressive front grille, larger air intakes, and quad exhaust tips, giving it a commanding road presence. Inside, the GLC 43 AMG offers premium materials, advanced technology, and a driver-focused cockpit with AMG-specific displays, it’s an ideal choice for those seeking a dynamic yet refined driving experience.


GLC performance:


The Mercedes-AMG GLC 43 delivers impressive performance, powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine that produces at least 421 horsepower and 500 Nm of torque. It features a 9-speed AMG Speedshift TCT automatic transmission, allowing for rapid and smooth gear changes. With AMG’s 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, the GLC 43 offers a rear-biased power distribution (31% front, 69% rear) for enhanced agility and cornering stability. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in around 4.8 seconds, making it one of the quickest SUVs in its class. The AMG-tuned adaptive air suspension (AMG Ride Control+) ensures a balance between comfort and sporty handling, while high-performance brakes provide strong stopping power. Combined with its AMG Dynamic Select driving modes, the GLC 43 can adapt to different driving conditions, offering an exhilarating yet controlled ride.
