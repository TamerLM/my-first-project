GLC 250 D:



The Mercedes-Benz GLC 250 d is a luxury diesel SUV that balances efficiency, performance, and comfort. It is powered by a 2.1-liter twin-turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine, producing 204 horsepower and 500 Nm of torque. Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission (9G-Tronic) and 4MATIC all-wheel drive, it provides smooth acceleration and excellent traction in various road conditions. The GLC 250 d can sprint from 0 to 100 km/h in around 7.6 seconds, making it a capable performer while maintaining impressive fuel efficiency.
